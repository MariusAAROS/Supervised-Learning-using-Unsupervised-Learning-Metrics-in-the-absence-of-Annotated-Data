Within 30 days after receiving an application from a person or legal entity for a benefit described in subsection (b), the Secretary shall request the Administrator of the Internal Revenue Service (referred to in this subsection as the `Administrator') to verify the average adjusted gross income, average adjusted gross farm income, and average adjusted gross nonfarm income of the person or legal entity.

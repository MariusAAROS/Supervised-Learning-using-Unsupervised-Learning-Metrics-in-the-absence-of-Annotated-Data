It shall be the policy of the United States to (1) undertake the necessary measures to deny the Cuban regime the financial resources to engage in activities that threaten (A) United States national security, its interests and its allies; (B) the environment and natural resources of the submerged lands of Cuba's northern coast and Florida's unique maritime environment; and (C) that prolong the dictatorship that oppresses the Cuban people; and (2) deter foreign investments that would enhance the ability of the Cuban regime to develop its petroleum resources.

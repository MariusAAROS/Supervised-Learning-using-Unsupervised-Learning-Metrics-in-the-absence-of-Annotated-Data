Any reduction in the basic pay of an individual referred to in subsection (b) of section 3011 of title 38, United States Code, by reason of such subsection, or of any individual referred to in subsection (c) of section 3012 of such title by reason of such subsection, shall cease commencing with months beginning on or after the date that is six months after the date of the enactment of this Act, and any obligation of such individual under such subsection (b) or (c), as the case may be, as of that date shall be deemed to be fully satisfied as of the date of the commencement of the first such month. An individual transferring entitlement to basic educational assistance under this section shall (1) designate the dependent or dependents to whom such entitlement is being transferred and the percentage of such entitlement to be transferred to each such dependent; and (2) specify the period for which the transfer shall be effective for each dependent designated under paragraph (1). (5) The purposes for which a dependent to whom entitlement is transferred under this section may use such entitlement shall include the pursuit and completion of the requirements of a secondary school diploma (or equivalency certificate). SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE. This Act may be cited as the Montgomery GI Bill Improvements Act of 2001. SEC.

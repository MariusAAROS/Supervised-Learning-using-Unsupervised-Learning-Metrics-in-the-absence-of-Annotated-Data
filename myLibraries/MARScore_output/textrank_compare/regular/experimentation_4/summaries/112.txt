Subsection (b) of section 179 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 (relating to limitations) is amended (A) by striking $25,000 ($125,000 in the case of taxable years beginning after 2006 and before 2011) in paragraph (1) and inserting $500,000, and (B) by striking $200,000 ($500,000 in the case of taxable years beginning after 2006 and before 2011) in paragraph (2) and inserting $1,000,000. The amendments made by this section shall apply to taxable years ending after the date of the enactment of this Act. The amendments made by this section shall apply to property placed in service in taxable years ending after the date of the enactment of this Act. SECTION 1.

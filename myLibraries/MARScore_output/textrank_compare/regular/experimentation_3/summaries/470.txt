Disallowance of deduction for certain advertising expenses for tobacco products. There is hereby appropriated to the AntiTobacco Use Trust Fund amounts equivalent to 50 percent of the increase in revenues to the Treasury attributable to section 280I (relating to disallowance of deduction for certain advertising expenses for tobacco products). With amounts available under section 9512(d) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 (relating to the AntiTobacco Use Trust Fund), the Secretary shall carry out a program of advertising to educate the public on the risks to health that result from the use of tobacco products.

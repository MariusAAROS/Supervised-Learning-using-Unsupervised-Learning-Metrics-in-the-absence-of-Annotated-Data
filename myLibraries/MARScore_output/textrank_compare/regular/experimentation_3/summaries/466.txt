(a) Securities Act of 1933.Section 17(a) of the Securities Act of 1933 (15 U.S.C. 77q(a)) is amended by striking securitybased swap agreement (as defined in section 206B of the GrammLeachBliley Act) and inserting swap agreement. Prior to taking action under subsection (a)(2), each Federal financial regulator shall consult, work, and cooperate with other Federal financial regulators to promote consistency in the treatment of swap agreements.

The Department of Justice shall maintain a nonpublic manual or computerized record of expungements under this subchapter containing only the name of, and alphanumeric identifiers selected by the Department of Justice that relate to, the persons who obtained expungement under this subchapter, and the order of expungement. Expungement of certain criminal records in limited circumstances (a) In General. Any individual convicted of a qualifying marijuanarelated offense who fulfills the requirements of section 3632 may, upon petition for expungement made in accordance with this subchapter, obtain an order granting expungement under this subchapter.

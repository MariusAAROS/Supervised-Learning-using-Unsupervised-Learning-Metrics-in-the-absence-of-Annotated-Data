The Secretary of the Interior shall publish in the Federal Register within 3 months after the date of enactment of this Act a list of all nonnative, human introduced bird species to which the Migratory Bird Treaty Act does not apply that belong to biological families of migratory birds covered under any of the migratory bird conventions with Great Britain (for Canada), Mexico, Russia, or Japan. Nothing in this section concerning the publication of the list shall delay implementation of other provisions of this Act that exclude nonnative, human introduced bird species from the application of the Migratory Bird Treaty Act.

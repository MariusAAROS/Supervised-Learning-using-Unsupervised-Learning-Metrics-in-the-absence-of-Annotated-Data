A thirdparty Internet sales site that in good faith monitors and removes any posting in accordance with paragraph (1) shall not be liable under any Federal or State law. If a person commits a violation of such subsection after two or more prior convictions of the person under this paragraph, the person shall for such violation be fined under such title or imprisoned not more than 10 years, or both. (2) The term `Internet' means collectively the myriad of computer and telecommunications facilities, including equipment and operating software, which comprise the interconnected worldwide network of networks that employ the transmission control protocol/internet protocol, or any predecessor or successor protocols to such protocol, to communicate information of all kinds by wire or radio. A thirdparty Internet sales site should monitor and remove in good faith any posting of an offer to sell an item that the thirdparty Internet sales site believes to violate Federal or State law, or that is the subject of a notice described in subsection (e). SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE. This Act may be cited as the Internet Drug Sales Accountability Act.

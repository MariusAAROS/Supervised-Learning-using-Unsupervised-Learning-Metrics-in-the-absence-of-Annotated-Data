The Secretary of the Interior shall make available to the Department of Energy, Marshall Islands Program, from funds available for the Technical Assistance Program of the Office of Insular Affairs, the amounts necessary to conduct the radiochemical analysis of groundwater under clause(i)(II). Effective beginning January 1, 2008, the Secretary of Energy shall, as a part of the Marshall Islands program conducted under subparagraph (A), periodically (but not less frequently than every 4 years) conduct (I) a visual study of the concrete exterior of the Cactus Crater containment structure on Runit Island; and (II) a radiochemical analysis of the groundwater surrounding and in the Cactus Crater containment structure on Runit Island.

some studies revealed a significant association between internet addiction and depressive symptoms in adolescents and high school students. moreover , the level of internet addiction correlated positively with the level of depression and suicidal ideation among high school students. studies showed that internet addiction positively correlated with depression , novelty seeking , harm avoidance , and reward dependence. in several studies it was demonstrated that significant factors affecting internet addiction were depression , gender , novelty seeking , and self transcendence ( 2 , 3 ). previous studies about internet addiction have investigated several associated psychological variables such as shyness , loneliness , self consciousness , anxiety , depression , and interpersonal relations ( 5 ). there were significant positive correlations among internet addiction , depression , and suicidal ideation in adolescents ( 7 ). the causes of internet addiction do not have only habitual bases , but also demographic and socioeconomic aspects.
